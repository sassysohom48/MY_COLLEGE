Things to Do Near KJSCE

Explore the Best Places Around KJSCE

KJSCE is located in Vidyavihar, Mumbai, a prime location with many great places to explore.

🍽️ **Popular Restaurants**: Enjoy delicious food at Shiv Sagar, Geeta Bhavan, and the famous Café Madras for authentic South Indian cuisine.

🛍️ **Shopping & Entertainment**: Spend time at **Phoenix Marketcity** and **R City Mall**, which offer everything from shopping to gaming and cinema halls.

🌊 **Beach & Marine Drive**: Head to **Juhu Beach** for Mumbai’s famous street food or enjoy the scenic beauty of **Marine Drive**, perfect for an evening walk.

🚋 **Tourist Attractions**: Explore the **Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus (CSMT)**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and visit **Gateway of India** for a historic experience.
