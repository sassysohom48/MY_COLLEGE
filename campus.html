Life at K.J. Somaiya College of Engineering

The K.J. Somaiya College of Engineering (KJSCE) campus is located in Vidyavihar, Mumbai. Spread over a vast area, the campus offers modern infrastructure, well-equipped labs, and a vibrant student community. From tech fests to cultural events, there is always something happening at KJSCE. With green spaces, sports facilities, and a well-connected location in the heart of Mumbai, students get a perfect balance of academics and extracurricular activities.
