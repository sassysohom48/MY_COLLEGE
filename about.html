K.J. Somaiya School of Engineering

K.J. Somaiya School of Engineering (KJSSE) is located in the heart of Mumbai, India. Established in 1995, it is a prominent educational institution offering a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering. The campus, which spans across a large area, offers state-of-the-art facilities and a vibrant environment for students to thrive academically and personally.
